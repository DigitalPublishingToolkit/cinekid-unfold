Dear Deer is an interactive poetry machine, which was a part of the Medialab at the Cinekid Festival 2016. The way Dear Deer works is that the viewer answers a few questions posed by the machine. Using this input, the machine creates a small entertaining poem, which comes scrolling on the screen. Inspired from this installation, we have created a similar format for displaying our interview sessions with the writer of Enter the Writers.
