Dear Deer is an interactive poetry machine created by Yu-Ting Feng, which was a part of the Medialab at the Cinekid Festival 2016.


This poetry machine, using inputs from the users, creates a small entertaining poem, which scrolls by on the screen.


Click on a keyword displayed on the screen and, based on your results, we provide you with the interviews with the writers displayed in a scrolling fashion, from bottom to top. It is similar to the way Dear Deer works! Enjoy!
