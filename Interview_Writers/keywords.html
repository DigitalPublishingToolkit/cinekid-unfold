Dear Deer is an interactive poetry machine, which was a part of the Medialab at the Cinekid Festival 2016. The way Dear Deer works is that the viewer answers a few questions posed by the machine. Using this input, the machine creates a small entertaining poem, which scrolls by on the screen. Inspired by this installation, we have created a similar format for displaying our interview sessions with the writers of Enter the Writers. Click on a keyword displayed on the screen and based on your results and the keywords, we provide you with the interviews in which the writers addressed the same topics. Their answers will be displayed in a scrolling fashion, from bottom to top, similar to the way Dear Deer works!
