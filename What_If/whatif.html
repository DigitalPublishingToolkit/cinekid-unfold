What if the internet went down? Can you imagine such a situation? Well, this question was posed to the kids at the Cinekid Festival, and they did a great job in drawing their creative imaginations of what they would do, if the internet goes down.


The kids were given two tasks: to explain how certain online tools work and to make a sketch explaining its workings. Then, the kids had to imagine an offline alternative for this online tool and to sketch it out.


The sketches were archived, and are displayed here!
