What if the internet went down? Can you imagine such a situation? Well, this question was posed to the kids at the Cinekid Festival, and they did a great job in drawing their creative imaginations of what they would do, if the internet goes down. This workshop was held at the Cinekid Festival to create awareness about the online world, and to encourage critical and independent thinking amongst the kids. The kids were given two tasks - to explain how certain online tools work and to make a sketch explaining its workings. Then, the kids had to imagine an offline alternative for this online tool and to sketch it out. The sketches were archived, and are displayed here.
